❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored exclusively on your device. There is no server, no account and no cloud sync. Your family stays private.

Do I need an account?

No. Kids Bucket List works without registration or login – just install and start creating memories.

When will the app be available?

The app is currently in development. The App Store release is planned for soon. Write to us if you'd like to be the first to know.

Which devices are supported?

Kids Bucket List will be available for iPhone and iPad running iOS/iPadOS.

Can I create custom categories?

Yes! In addition to the 13 built-in categories you can create your own at any time and personalise them with a colour and icon.
