What the app does

Everything your family needs

Thoughtful. Personal. For everyone in the family.

📋

Family Bucket List

Create activities, assign them to children and check them off together – from adventures to family traditions.

💭

Capture Memories

Add the date, a personal note and a rating – stars or emojis – when you complete an activity.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Profiles for Every Child

Individual profiles with avatar and favourite colour – switch between family overview and individual view instantly.

🗂️

14 Specialised Categories

Each category has its own experience – search books directly, track films, discover theme parks.

📍

Discover Nearby

Find playgrounds, day-trip destinations and theme parks near you – perfect for the next spontaneous outing.

📊

Statistics & Highlights

Charts, top categories and personalised recommendations – your progress and favourite moments at a glance.

☁️

Optional iCloud Sync

Enable iCloud Sync and access your bucket list across all your Apple devices – seamlessly between iPhone, iPad and Mac. Your data stays private in your own iCloud.
